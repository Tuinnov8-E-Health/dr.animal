Our Story
About Doctor
Animal Auto

Founded in 2010 in Githunguri, Doctor Animal Auto started as a small two-bay workshop with one mechanic and a dream: to give every Kenyan driver access to honest, expert automotive care.

Today, we operate a 20-bay facility with over 30 certified technicians, modern OBD diagnostic equipment, and a digital client management system that keeps you informed at every step.

We believe your car is more than a machine — it's your livelihood, your freedom, your family's safety. That's why we treat every vehicle with surgical precision and genuine care.

Certified by Kenya Motor Industry Association

Authorized dealer for Toyota, Nissan & Mitsubishi parts

6-month labour warranty on all repairs

24-hour emergency breakdown support
